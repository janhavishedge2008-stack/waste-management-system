Recycling & Sustainability

Our Recycling Methods

We employ state-of-the-art recycling technologies to ensure maximum material recovery and minimal environmental impact.

Paper Recycling

Paper is pulped, cleaned, and reformed into new paper products, saving trees and energy.

Plastic Recycling

Plastics are sorted, cleaned, melted, and reformed into new products or materials.

Metal Recycling

Metals are melted down and recast, requiring less energy than mining new materials.
